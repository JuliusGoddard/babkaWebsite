About us

Babka is an independent bakery in the heart of Tollcross.
We offer a range of products including our famous beigels, sourdoughs, cakes, coffees, soups and, of course, babka!
Everything is made on site by a small team who really love bread!

Cookies and Cakes

For those desiring a sweet option, aside from our daily babka, cookies and cakes are prepared on a rotational basis, including (but not limited to!) marble cake, honey cake, a variety of tarts, and cinnamon buns!

All handmade and freshly baked

All products are baked and made on site. Our daily breads comprise of our famous beigels, white and seeded sourdoughs, dark and caraway rye sourdoughs, baguettes, and Friday-only chollah!

Pickle jars and home-made kombucha

We are pleased to offer a selection of home-made fermented goods, as added extras in our filled beigels or jarred for use at home. Browse our half-sour pickle coins, pickled onions, sauerkraut and mustard in store, or enjoy an iced home-made kombucha!
